Meet Oana – aka Wanna

Oana (pronounced "Wanna" – yes, that's where we got the name) is the heart, soul, and sparkle behind Wanna Pilates.

With her contagious energy, eagle-eye for form, and zero tolerance for boring workouts, Oana started Wanna Pilates for people like her.

A former ballet student turned Pilates powerhouse, Oana first discovered Pilates during her classical dance training. She was drawn to the way it helped her build strength, flexibility, and control—all while healing injuries and creating balance in her body. That mind-body connection? Instant love.

What is Wanna Pilates?

Wanna Pilates is more than a studio, it's a movement method. A place where movement becomes a language for every body. A place to move, to breathe and connect.

From the moment you arrive, you're invited to leave the outside world behind. Whether you're seeking strength, flexibility, or simply a moment of peace, our classes are designed to meet you exactly where you are. We believe in the power of movement as healing, the magic of community, and the beauty of the human body in motion.

Our philosophy is simple: every body is different, and every journey deserves care. That's why our approach is rooted in compassion, precision, and play - three elements that work together to create transformative movement.
